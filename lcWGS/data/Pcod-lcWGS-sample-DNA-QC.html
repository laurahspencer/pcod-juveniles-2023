4.Note


4.1 Sample classification standard table

Notes：
The test conclusion is a comprehensive evaluation based on Novogene’s sample quality requirements for library preparation and sequencing
The test result based on the “DNA Samples QC Criteria”explains whether the testing sample meets the requirement of library construction.
a) Pass: The sample is qualified and the library preparation can be prepared regularly;
b) Hold: The sample does not totally meet the requirements of library construction and sequencing. Novogene can try to construct library but the sequencing quality is not guaranteed;
c) Fail: It is highly recommended for clients to resend the sample or the library could be constructed at high risk and the sequencing quality is not guaranteed (not recommended).

4.2 Sample volume

If the incoming sample volume is less than 10 μL, 15 μL of EB will be added into the sample to ensure sufficient total volume for both sample QC and downstream processes.
